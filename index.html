Tours & Trips

3-Day Lenana Summit Trek

This express trek via the Sirimon route is ideal for those with limited time but high stamina. Hike through forest, moorland, and alpine zones. Expect early alpine starts, stunning sunrise views from Point Lenana, and safe, guided descents. Includes permits, meals, and porter service.

5-Day Chogoria Route Experience

The most scenic route up Mt. Kenya, passing Lake Ellis, Lake Michaelson, and the dramatic Gorges Valley. Enjoy longer acclimatization, wildlife sightings, and epic photography moments. Includes camping gear, meals, certified guides, and a vehicle pickup at the base.

Kilimanjaro – Marangu Route (5 Days)

An affordable, popular route with hut accommodation. Great for beginners aiming for Uhuru Peak.

Kilimanjaro – Machame Route (7 Days)

A challenging, scenic trek through five ecological zones. Ideal for fit climbers seeking adventure.

Maasai Mara Safari (3 Days)

Witness the Big Five and the Great Migration in Kenya’s most famous reserve. Full-board game drive experience.

Samburu Cultural Safari (3 Days)

Discover rare northern wildlife and authentic Samburu culture in this unique semi-arid reserve.

Nairobi National Park Day Trip

Enjoy a full-day game drive just minutes from the city. Perfect for layovers or short stays.

About Patrick

Patrick Thuita is a certified Wilderness First Responder with a Diploma in guiding and over 6 years of experience leading tours on Mt. Kenya. He has completed 105+ summits, including 4 on Kilimanjaro, and holds the President's Award Silver Level.
